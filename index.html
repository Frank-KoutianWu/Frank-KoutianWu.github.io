    Currently, I am doing reserch called Meteor shower velocity estimates from the Mengcheng meteor radar under the guidance of Prof. Xue Xianghui and research called Study the evolution of CME using the in situ observations near the Earth and Mars under the guidance of Prof. Shen Chenglong.

  • Meteor shower velocity estimates from the Mengcheng meteor radar

      • Abstract:

      Meteor radar is an important space detection method, which was mainly used in meteor shower, meteor velocity observation and related astronomical problems at the beginning. Modern all-sky meteor radar systems mainly detect background physical parameters such as horizontal wind field, temperature and density in the middle and upper atmosphere.

      The function of meteor radar to observe the physical parameters of meteoroids has been neglected. This research project intends to use the meteorological radar observation of the Mengcheng National Field Observatory of the University of Science and Technology of China to detect the speed and spatial information of meteors entering the earth's atmosphere to realize the observation and statistical analysis of meteor showers. And by using the statistical results of meteor shower observations, the reliability of meteor radar to detect meteor shower phenomenon is discussed.

      Key words: meteor radar; upper atmosphere; meteor shower; meteor velocity

      Main majors: Astronomy; Space physics

  • Study the evolution of CME using the in situ observations near the Earth and Mars

      • Abstract:

      日冕物质抛射 (CME) 的传播过程研究是空间物理和空间天气中的关键问题之一，而日冕物质抛射内部磁场和等离子特征的研究将加深我们对日冕物质抛射形成和演化的认识。而以前对日冕物质抛射内部特性的研究多基于单点的局地探测，本课题将利用行星探测计划带来的不同距离处对日冕物质抛射内部结构的观测，开展日冕物质抛射内部结构特征演化研究。

      Key words: CME; in situ

      Main majors: Space physics; Space weather
